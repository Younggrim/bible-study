What the Bible Says About Addiction

"So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed."

— John 8:36 (ESV)

Key Scriptures

Romans 6:14

"For sin will have no dominion over you, since you are not under law but under grace."

1 Corinthians 6:12

"All things are lawful for me, but not all things are helpful. I will not be dominated by anything."

Galatians 5:1

"For freedom Christ has set us free; stand firm therefore, and do not submit again to a yoke of slavery."

2 Peter 2:19

"For whatever overcomes a person, to that he is enslaved."

Romans 8:2

"For the law of the Spirit of life has set you free in Christ Jesus from the law of sin and death."

Philippians 4:13

"I can do all things through him who strengthens me."

What Scripture Teaches

Addiction is slavery — but Christ came to set captives free. Freedom is possible, though it may be a process.

True deliverance comes through the power of the Spirit replacing old desires with new ones.

Isolation fuels addiction. Bring your struggle into the light with trusted believers.

Recovery is not linear. Relapse is not the end — get back up. God's mercies are new every morning.
